Dog-dentifier Project


Predicting a Dogs Breed and Recommending the Best Fit

For this project I used a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to predict dogs breeds from their photos. If you upload multiple photos, you can fill out a survey and use a content-based recommender system to help find which dog best fits your needs!

I had an absolute blast making this project and learned a lot about neural nets, flask and web scraping in the process
